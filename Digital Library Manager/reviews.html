The Midnight Library

Matt Haig

4.5/5

A beautiful and thought-provoking novel about the choices we make and the lives we could have lived. Nora Seed finds herself in a library between life and death, with each book representing a different version of her life.

Andy Weir

5/5

An incredible sci-fi adventure that combines hard science with heart. The protagonist wakes up alone on a spaceship with no memory of who he is or his mission, and must save humanity from extinction.

James Clear

4/5

This book changed my perspective on habit formation. Clear provides practical strategies for building good habits and breaking bad ones. The concept of 1% improvements really resonated with me.

The Song of Achilles

Madeline Miller

4.5/5

A beautiful retelling of the Iliad from Patroclus' perspective. Miller's prose is lyrical and captivating, bringing ancient Greece to life. The relationship between Achilles and Patroclus is portrayed with such tenderness.

Emma Thompson
